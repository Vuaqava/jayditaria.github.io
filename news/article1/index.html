President Dagsa welcomes all to the new Jayditarian website

Published 01/20/21 Jayditarian Standard Time (UTC+8),
and of the independence of Jayditaria, the 2nd.

Jayditarian State News

a the Republic of Jayditaria, and Minister for Information and Communications Technology, welcomes all to the brand new Jayditarian website, in a statement today.

Dear visitors and citizens of the Republic,
I welcome you all to the new website of our government, developed by our great Ministry for Information and Communications Technology.
As you wander through our website, may you be inspired to join us and be part of the great country built on our motto: "Libertas sit amet, non voluntariis" ("Freedom is mandatory, not voluntary.")

May God bless our Republic and all of us.
