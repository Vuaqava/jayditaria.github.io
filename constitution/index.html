Please note that this is an incomplete constitution. It will still be updated until further notice.


Preamble

We, the sovereign nation of the Republic of Jayditaria, in order to establish a Republic
wherein all rights of the people are guaranteed, freedom is mandatory, and choice is not
an option, but compulsory, do promulgate and ratify this Constitution, to establish a
nation built on liberty, equality and fraternity, and the guaranteed protection of the basic
rights and freedoms of man.
